Daniel ‘dp’ Pedraza

Long version: Daniel is from Mexico. He is the PM for equalAIs and ecstatic to return and help implement tools against pervasive surveillance and protect civil liberties. Daniel has a background in aerospace engineering and started his career as a Formula One aerodynamicist. He is currently supporting customer development efforts and contributing to product strategy at Berbix – a YC startup building identity verification and fraud deterrence as a service. Daniel has spent time looking at the open challenges to harnessing technology for public good, previously serving the United Nations. As a child he dreamt of becoming an astronaut but never made it into space.
